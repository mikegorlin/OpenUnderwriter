Stock drops shown in the worksheet must include an explanation of WHY the drop happened.

**Why:** A bare stock drop percentage is meaningless for D&O underwriting. The causal event is what matters — was it an earnings miss, SEC investigation, product recall, guidance withdrawal, insider selling? The cause determines SCA exposure. A -15% drop from broad market selloff is very different from -15% after restating financials.

**How to apply:**
- For each significant stock drop (e.g., >5% single-day), cross-reference:
  - SEC filings around that date (8-K, 10-Q, proxy amendments)
  - News articles from that date window (±2 days)
  - Earnings dates (was it a post-earnings drop?)
- Display the causal event alongside the drop magnitude
- This is a critical enrichment for the ACQUIRE/EXTRACT stages

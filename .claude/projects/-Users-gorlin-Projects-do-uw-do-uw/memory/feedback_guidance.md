EPS guidance column must show the company's OWN guidance, not analyst consensus estimates.

**Why:** Analyst consensus is a different data point. For D&O underwriting, what matters is: does the company guide? How detailed is guidance (revenue, EPS, segments)? How frequently do they update? Have they narrowed/widened/withdrawn? Is guidance consistent or volatile? Companies that don't guide (like Apple) should show "No guidance provided" — not consensus estimates masquerading as guidance.

**How to apply:**
- Distinguish company guidance (8-K, earnings call transcripts) from analyst consensus (yfinance estimates)
- Track: guidance detail level (EPS only? Revenue? Segments?), update frequency, historical changes (raised/lowered/maintained/withdrawn), consistency
- Show analyst consensus separately (it's useful but different)
- "No guidance provided" is a meaningful D&O signal — some companies deliberately avoid guidance to reduce SCA exposure

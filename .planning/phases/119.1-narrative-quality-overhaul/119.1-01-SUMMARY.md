## Decisions Made
- neg_from_finding replaces neg_generic as fallback: extracts signal data from finding's section_origin and evidence_narrative, looks up factor scores, builds company-specific text. If no signal data available, uses raw evidence_narrative directly (still better than generic boilerplate).
- Stock decline severity thresholds calibrated to actual market ranges: >=70% catastrophic, >=50% severe, >=30% substantial, >=15% significant, <15% moderate pullback.
- pos_stable_leadership uses both tenure AND company health (Altman Z + scoring tier) to differentiate: 13-year CEO at healthy company gets "leadership continuity demonstrates management competence" while 2-year CEO at distressed company gets "limited track record for evaluating management's response to adversity".

```python
COMMENTARY_RULES = (
    "You are a senior D&O underwriter writing a formal research report section.\n"
    "Return TWO clearly labeled sections:\n\n"
    "WHAT WAS SAID:\n"
    "[2-3 sentences. ONLY facts from the data provided: dollar amounts, percentages, dates, names. "
    "No interpretation. No opinion. Third-person, formal tone. Example: "
    "'Revenue reached $394.3B in FY2024, up 2.0% year-over-year, with net income of $101.0B.']\n\n"
    "UNDERWRITING COMMENTARY:\n"
    "[3-6 bullet points. Each bullet: one D&O risk finding with specific numbers and the "
    "SCA litigation theory it maps to. Reference theories by name: Section 10(b)/Rule 10b-5, "
    "loss causation (Dura Pharmaceuticals), scienter (Tellabs), Section 11 strict liability, "
    "PSLRA safe harbor, Caremark duty of oversight, duty of candor. "
    "Format each bullet as: '- [Finding with specific data]: [SCA theory implication]']\n\n"
    "Rules:\n"
    "- Every sentence MUST contain company-specific data from the provided context.\n"
    "- In WHAT WAS SAID: zero interpretation, zero opinion. Pure facts.\n"
    "- In UNDERWRITING COMMENTARY: every bullet connects a finding to a named SCA theory.\n"
    "- NEVER reference scoring factors (F1-F10), signal IDs, check ratios, composite scores, "
    "deduction points, or any scoring engine terminology.\n"
    "- NEVER use phrases like 'AI Assessment', 'automated analysis', 'system detected', "
    "'brain', 'data coverage', 'signal triggered'.\n"
    "- Write as if a human senior underwriter authored this. Formal research report tone.\n"
    "- No hedging. No generic statements. No filler.\n"
)
```

Create per-section prompt builders following the same `_financial_prompt`, `_market_prompt`, etc. pattern from `narrative_prompts.py` but with commentary-specific instructions. Each builder takes `(company_name: str, data_str: str)` and returns a prompt string. Map them in `COMMENTARY_PROMPT_BUILDERS: dict[str, Callable]`.

Export `build_commentary_prompt(section_id: str, company_name: str, data_str: str) -> str` that dispatches to the right builder.

Per-section prompt specifics:
- **financial**: Request revenue/income facts in WHAT WAS SAID; D&O relevance of Altman Z/Beneish/restatement risk in COMMENTARY (name the theory: going-concern allegations, restatement exposure)
- **market**: Stock price/drops/insider selling facts; loss causation arguments, corrective disclosure timeline, scienter from insider activity
- **governance**: Board size/independence/comp facts; Caremark duty of oversight, duty of candor, board liability theories
- **litigation**: Active SCA count/settlement history facts; recurrence probability, settlement benchmarking, coverage implications
- **scoring**: Quality score and tier facts; overall risk profile characterization, peer-relative positioning
- **company**: Business description/sector/MCap facts; sector-specific SCA patterns, business model litigation exposure
- **executive_brief**: Aggregate key facts; overarching risk narrative connecting all findings
- **meeting_prep**: Key risk areas facts; underwriter questions with SCA theory grounding

For each section, the LLM prompt MUST include the actual data:

1. **Financial Health narrative** (`pre_computed_narratives.financial`):
   - Include: revenue/net income/EPS with $ amounts, debt ratio, current ratio, Altman Z-Score value+zone, Beneish M-Score value+zone, Piotroski F-Score value+zone, Ohlson O-Score value+zone, all forensic indicator values, key financial ratios
   - From: `state.extracted.financials`, `state.scoring.factor_scores[F3]`, signal results for FIN.* prefix
   - Must tell the STORY: "RPM's financial profile shows..." not "This section covers financial health"

2. **Market Events narrative** (`pre_computed_narratives.market`):
   - Include: stock price (current, 52w high/low, % decline), number/magnitude of significant drops, insider selling volume ($ and % of cap), executive departures (names, dates, types), any cluster events
   - From: `state.extracted.market_data`, `state.scoring.factor_scores[F2, F7]`

3. **Governance narrative** (`pre_computed_narratives.governance`):
   - Include: board size, independence %, avg tenure, key governance red flags, CEO name+tenure, compensation structure, prior litigation per executive
   - From: `state.extracted.governance`, `state.scoring.factor_scores[F6]`

4. **Litigation narrative** (`pre_computed_narratives.litigation`):
   - Include: active SCA count+names, settlement history (amounts, dates), lead counsel tier, SEC enforcement actions, sector SCA filing rate comparison, claim probability estimate
   - From: `state.extracted.litigation`, `state.scoring.factor_scores[F1, F5, F9]`

5. **Scoring narrative** (`pre_computed_narratives.scoring`):
   - Include: quality score, tier, top 3 contributing factors with deductions, any red flag ceilings, tier explanation
   - From: `state.scoring`

6. **Company narrative** (`pre_computed_narratives.company`):
   - Include: company name, ticker, sector, market cap, employees, years public, SIC code, primary business description
   - From: `state.company`

Each prompt MUST:
- Require company-specific data in every sentence (QUAL-04)
- Require dollar amounts, percentages, dates, names (QUAL-01)
- Be 3-5 sentences
- Tell the STORY of risk, not describe the section
- Reference scoring factors where relevant (QUAL-02)

    The script processes every check in brain/checks.json and adds 4 new fields based on the rules in check_classification.json:

    **Category assignment rules** (from research + CONTEXT.md decisions):
    - `DECISION_DRIVING`: Checks that change tier assignment, trigger CRF, or materially affect the underwriting decision. These are SCORED (factors non-empty). Primarily: STOCK.PRICE.*, STOCK.SHORT.*, STOCK.INSIDER.*, FIN.ACCT.*, FIN.LIQ.*, FIN.DEBT.*, FIN.GUIDE.*, LIT.SCA.*, LIT.REG.*, GOV.INSIDER.*, GOV.ACTIVIST.*, FWRD.WARN.* with factor mappings, FWRD.EVENT.* with factor mappings.
    - `CONTEXT_DISPLAY`: Useful information always displayed but NOT scored. Primarily: BIZ.CLASS.*, BIZ.SIZE.*, BIZ.MODEL.* (structural info), BIZ.DEPEND.* (many informational), BIZ.COMP.* (many informational), BIZ.UNI.* (informational), GOV.BOARD.* (baseline governance), GOV.RIGHTS.* (structural), GOV.PAY.* (informational compensation), GOV.EXEC.tenure/experience (informational), checks with threshold type "info", "classification", "search".
    - `FUTURE_RESEARCH`: Checks that need data sources not yet available. Primarily: checks requiring MANUAL_ONLY that have no realistic automation path.

    **Plaintiff lens assignment rules** (from research plaintiff lens coverage):
    Use the prefix_defaults from check_classification.json. For checks not covered by a prefix, derive from the check's section + factor combination:
    - Section 2 (STOCK): SHAREHOLDERS default
    - Section 3 (FIN): depends on sub-prefix — ACCT/GUIDE → SHAREHOLDERS+REGULATORS, LIQ/DEBT → CREDITORS+SHAREHOLDERS
    - Section 4 (LIT): depends on sub-prefix — SCA → SHAREHOLDERS, REG → REGULATORS, OTHER → varies
    - Section 5 (GOV): SHAREHOLDERS default, INSIDER → +REGULATORS
    - Section 6 (FWRD): SHAREHOLDERS default

    **Signal type assignment:**
    - STRUCTURAL: BIZ.CLASS, BIZ.SIZE, BIZ.MODEL, GOV.RIGHTS, GOV.BOARD (baseline)
    - LEVEL: FIN.LIQ, FIN.DEBT, FIN.PROF (point-in-time financial metrics)
    - DELTA: STOCK.PRICE, FIN.GUIDE, any check measuring CHANGE
    - FORENSIC: FIN.ACCT (accounting manipulation detection)
    - EVENT: LIT.SCA, LIT.REG, FWRD.EVENT, GOV.ACTIVIST (discrete events)
    - PATTERN: checks with threshold_type "pattern"
    - NLP: (reserved for new NLP checks in Plan 05)

    **Hazard/Signal assignment:**
    - HAZARD: Persistent structural conditions (BIZ.*, GOV.RIGHTS.*, GOV.BOARD baseline)
    - SIGNAL: Transient behavioral evidence (STOCK.*, FIN.ACCT.*, FIN.GUIDE.*, GOV.INSIDER.*, FWRD.WARN.*)

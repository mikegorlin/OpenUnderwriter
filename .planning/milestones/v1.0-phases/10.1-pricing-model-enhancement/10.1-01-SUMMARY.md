# Phase 10.1 Plan 01: Enhanced ORM and Pydantic Models Summary

**One-liner:** Program/PolicyYear/Broker/Carrier ORM hierarchy with LayerType/DataCompleteness/DataSource enums and partial-data Pydantic models accepting just a ticker.

## What Was Done

### Task 1: ORM Models and TowerLayer Enhancement
Created the new `pricing_models_program.py` with four ORM models following the Program -> PolicyYear -> TowerLayer hierarchy:

- **Broker** (`brokers` table): brokerage_name required, producer/email/phone optional
- **Carrier** (`carriers` table): carrier_name required with index, am_best_rating and appetite optional
- **Program** (`programs` table): ticker-only minimum viable record (D2 decision), FK to broker, cascade to policy_years
- **PolicyYear** (`policy_years` table): program_id + policy_year required, all pricing fields nullable, defaults to QUOTED status and FRAGMENT completeness

Enhanced TowerLayer with 6 new nullable columns: layer_type, layer_label, commission_pct, data_source, carrier_id (FK to carriers), policy_year_id (FK to policy_years). All backward-compatible -- existing data unaffected.

Added import registration in `pricing_models.py` to ensure FK target tables exist when `Base.metadata.create_all` is called, resolving SQLAlchemy's `NoReferencedTableError` for cross-module FK dependencies.

### Task 2: Pydantic Models and Enums
Added three new StrEnum types:
- **LayerType**: PRIMARY, EXCESS, SIDE_A (D1 tower semantics)
- **DataCompleteness**: FRAGMENT, PARTIAL, COMPLETE (data quality tracking)
- **DataSource**: VERIFIED, INFERRED, ESTIMATED, AI_EXTRACTED (D7 transparency)

Created 10 new Pydantic models:
- BrokerInput/BrokerOutput, CarrierInput/CarrierOutput
- EnhancedLayerInput (partial data: nullable limit, premium, carrier)
- PolicyYearInput/PolicyYearOutput (defaults to FRAGMENT completeness)
- ProgramInput/ProgramOutput (minimum viable = just ticker)

All existing QuoteInput/QuoteOutput/TowerLayerInput/TowerLayerOutput models unchanged.

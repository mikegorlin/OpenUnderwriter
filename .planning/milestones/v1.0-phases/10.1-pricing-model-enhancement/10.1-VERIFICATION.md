# Phase 10.1: Pricing Model Enhancement Verification Report

**Phase Goal:** The pricing database schema is reworked to reflect how D&O insurance market intelligence actually arrives in the real world — partial data is the norm, not the exception. An underwriter might know only a renewal date, or just one layer's pricing, or the broker but not the terms. The enhanced model supports: partial/incomplete program records that are still valuable, year-over-year tracking showing how programs evolve (carrier rotations, broker changes, pricing movement), broker and brokerage capture with contact information, proper policy year management, and flexible data entry where any combination of fields is useful. Critically, the system can ingest real-world artifacts — quota share binders, policies, program summaries, Excel tower spreadsheets — and extract pricing intelligence from them. The analytics engine is updated to work with this richer, more realistic data model.

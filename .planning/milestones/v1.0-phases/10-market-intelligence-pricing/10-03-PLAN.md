<objective>
Integrate market pricing intelligence into the analysis pipeline so that the BENCHMARK stage cross-references the system's risk assessment with actual market pricing data, surfacing mispricing alerts when significant divergence is detected.

Purpose: This is the payoff -- the system can now tell underwriters "the market is pricing this risk at X but our analysis suggests Y" with statistical backing. Without this integration, pricing data lives in isolation from the analytical pipeline.

Output: BenchmarkStage optionally enriches AnalysisState with market intelligence data (market position, mispricing alerts, carrier landscape) when pricing data exists. Non-breaking when no data is available.
</objective>

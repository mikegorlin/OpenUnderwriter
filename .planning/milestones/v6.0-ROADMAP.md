### v6.0 Company Profile Completeness (Phases 93-100)

**Milestone Goal:** Close the company profile gap -- every dimension that D&O underwriters evaluate beyond governance/litigation/financials must be extracted, analyzed, and rendered. The worksheet's Business Profile section should match institutional underwriting depth.

**Architectural Constraint:** Every new data dimension MUST be expressed as brain signals (`brain/signals/*.yaml`) with acquisition, evaluation (thresholds), and presentation blocks. Signals wired into output manifest for render dispatch. No data enters the worksheet outside the brain signal framework.

- [x] **Phase 93: Business Model Extraction** - Extract revenue model, concentration risk, key person, lifecycle, disruption, and margin data from 10-K (completed 2026-03-10)
- [x] **Phase 94: Operational Data Extraction** - Extract subsidiary structure, workforce distribution, and operational resilience indicators (completed 2026-03-10)
- [x] **Phase 95: Corporate Event Extraction** - Extract M&A history, IPO/offering windows, restatements, capital changes, and render existing business_changes (completed 2026-03-10)
- [x] **Phase 96: Structural Complexity Extraction** - Extract disclosure complexity, non-GAAP usage, related parties, off-balance-sheet, and holding structure (completed 2026-03-10)
- [x] **Phase 97: External Environment Assessment** - Extract regulatory intensity, geopolitical exposure, ESG gaps, cyber risk, and macro sensitivity (completed 2026-03-10)
- [x] **Phase 98: Sector Risk Classification** - Build sector hazard tiers, claim pattern reference data, sector regulatory overlay, and peer risk comparison (completed 2026-03-10)
- [x] **Phase 99: Operational Scoring & Signals** - Render operational complexity indicators and compute unified operational complexity score (completed 2026-03-10)
- [x] **Phase 100: Display Integration & Output** - Render all new dimensions in Business Profile, restructure section layout, add complexity dashboard, update Word/PDF (completed 2026-03-10)

## Phase Details

### Phase 93: Business Model Extraction
**Goal**: Underwriters can see a complete business model profile -- revenue model type, concentration risks, key person dependency, segment lifecycle stages, disruption exposure, and margin profile -- all expressed as brain signals with evaluation thresholds
**Depends on**: Nothing (first phase of v6.0)
**Requirements**: BMOD-01, BMOD-02, BMOD-03, BMOD-04, BMOD-05, BMOD-06
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. 6+ new brain signals in `brain/signals/biz/` with acquisition, evaluation, and presentation blocks (BIZ.MODEL.revenue_type, BIZ.MODEL.concentration_risk, BIZ.MODEL.key_person, BIZ.MODEL.lifecycle, BIZ.MODEL.disruption, BIZ.MODEL.segment_margins)
  2. Revenue concentration risk signal fires (TRIGGERED) when single segment >50%, single customer >10%, or single geography >40%
  3. Key person dependency signal evaluates founder-led, CEO tenure, succession plan with red/amber/green thresholds
  4. All signals wired to output manifest and render through facet dispatch
  5. `underwrite TICKER --fresh` populates all business model signal results in state
**Plans:** 2/2 plans complete

- [x] 93-02-PLAN.md — Wire signal evaluation, output manifest, context builder, and tests

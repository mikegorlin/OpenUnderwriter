## 📋 FRAMEWORK OVERVIEW

### Purpose
Comprehensive risk assessment framework for D&O liability insurance underwriting of public companies. Systematically evaluates litigation risk, financial health, business model sustainability, governance quality, market dynamics, and alternative data signals.

### Structure
- **PART 1: QUICK SCREEN** (43 checks, QS-1 through QS-43) - 5-10 minute triage
- **PART 2: COMPREHENSIVE ANALYSIS** (534 checks, numbered 1-534) - Full underwriting analysis

### How to Use
1. **Always start with Quick Screen** (Part 1)
2. **If 3+ red flags in Quick Screen** → DECLINE immediately, document findings
3. **If <3 red flags** → Proceed to Comprehensive Analysis (Part 2)
4. **Stop early if sufficient evidence** to decline during comprehensive analysis
5. **Verify all claims with 2+ sources** (especially soft signals)

### Color Coding
- 🟢 **GREEN** = Pass / Low Risk / Compliant
- 🟡 **YELLOW** = Caution / Moderate Risk / Needs Monitoring  
- 🔴 **RED** = Fail / High Risk / Critical Issue
- 🟣 **PURPLE** = Unknown / Insufficient Data / Requires Manual Research

### LITIGATION & REGULATORY (8 checks)

#### QS-1: Active Securities Class Action
- **Check**: Active securities class action lawsuit filed within past 24 months
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + estimated settlement >$50M OR >10% of market cap
- **Sources**: Stanford Securities Clearinghouse, company 8-K, 10-K/10-Q Legal Proceedings

#### QS-2: SEC Enforcement Action  
- **Check**: Active SEC enforcement action or settlement within past 12 months
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + penalties >$25M OR officer-and-director bars
- **Sources**: SEC.gov Litigation Releases, company 8-K

#### QS-3: DOJ Criminal Investigation
- **Check**: Active DOJ criminal investigation or charges against company or executives
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + criminal charges filed OR target letter disclosed
- **Sources**: DOJ press releases, company 8-K, news reports

#### QS-4: Multiple Derivative Lawsuits
- **Check**: 2+ active derivative lawsuits with Caremark duty of oversight claims
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + parallel securities class action
- **Sources**: Delaware Court of Chancery, PACER, company disclosures

#### QS-5: Financial Restatement + SEC Investigation
- **Check**: Financial restatement announced + SEC investigation disclosed
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + restatement >5% of revenue OR >10% of net income
- **Sources**: Company 8-K Item 4.02, SEC comment letters

#### QS-6: Auditor Resignation
- **Check**: Auditor resigned (not dismissed) within past 12 months
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + cited disagreement OR fraud concerns
- **Sources**: Company 8-K Item 4.01

#### QS-7: Bankruptcy Filing
- **Check**: Chapter 11 bankruptcy filed OR Chapter 7 liquidation
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes (any bankruptcy filing)
- **Sources**: Company 8-K, PACER bankruptcy court records

#### QS-8: FCPA Criminal Charges
- **Check**: DOJ FCPA criminal charges filed against company or executives
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + criminal charges OR penalty >$100M
- **Sources**: DOJ FCPA enforcement database, company 8-K

#### QS-9: Going Concern Warning
- **Check**: Auditor issued going concern warning in most recent audit
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + cash runway <6 months

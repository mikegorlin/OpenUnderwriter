## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Cassava Sciences presents **EXTREME HIGH RISK** for D&O liability insurance underwriting. The company is currently defending multiple active securities class action lawsuits with an estimated settlement contingency of $31.25 million recorded in Q2 2025. The company faces allegations of scientific misconduct, data manipulation, and misleading investors regarding its lead Alzheimer's drug candidate simufilam. Following FDA rejection and clinical trial termination, the company has pivoted to epilepsy indications with only preclinical data. Cash position of $112.4 million provides approximately 18-20 months of runway at current burn rates, creating financial pressure. Short interest remains elevated, and the company has been the subject of intense short seller activism and regulatory scrutiny.

**UNDERWRITING RECOMMENDATION**: **DECLINE** or price at severe premium with restrictive terms, high retentions, and carved-out exclusions for ongoing litigation.

# Securities Litigation by Industry Sector - Summary Data

## Source
Cornerstone Research - Securities Class Action Settlements 2014-2023

## Industry Sector Settlement Patterns (2014-2023)

### Financial Services
- **Number of Settlements**: 91 cases
- **Median Settlement**: $17.8 million
- **Median Simplified Tiered Damages**: $313.3 million
- **Settlement as % of Damages**: 5.3%
- **Notes**: Highest median settlement amount; firms in financial sector consistently report higher total assets than other defendants; over 15% of 2023 settlements involved financial sector (highest in decade)

### Technology
- **Number of Settlements**: 106 cases
- **Median Settlement**: $9.4 million
- **Median Simplified Tiered Damages**: $318.2 million
- **Settlement as % of Damages**: 4.3%
- **Notes**: Second-largest number of cases; includes software, semiconductors, hardware, and emerging tech

### Pharmaceuticals
- **Number of Settlements**: 122 cases (MOST CASES)
- **Median Settlement**: $8.5 million
- **Median Simplified Tiered Damages**: $242.5 million
- **Settlement as % of Damages**: 3.9%
- **Notes**: Highest volume of cases; lower settlement percentages suggest more cases dismissed or settled for smaller amounts

### Retail
- **Number of Settlements**: 51 cases
- **Median Settlement**: $15.2 million
- **Median Simplified Tiered Damages**: $350.4 million
- **Settlement as % of Damages**: 4.6%
- **Notes**: Second-highest median settlement; includes consumer products and retail operations

### Telecommunications
- **Number of Settlements**: 28 cases
- **Median Settlement**: $11.4 million
- **Median Simplified Tiered Damages**: $381.0 million
- **Settlement as % of Damages**: 4.4%
- **Notes**: Fewer cases but significant damages when they occur

### Healthcare
- **Number of Settlements**: 21 cases
- **Median Settlement**: $10.1 million
- **Median Simplified Tiered Damages**: $240.4 million
- **Settlement as % of Damages**: 6.0% (HIGHEST)
- **Notes**: Highest settlement percentage relative to damages; includes healthcare services, medical devices, hospitals

## Key Insights for Underwriting

### Highest Risk Industries (by volume)
1. Pharmaceuticals (122 cases)
2. Technology (106 cases)
3. Financial Services (91 cases)

### Highest Settlement Amounts
1. Financial Services ($17.8M median)
2. Retail ($15.2M median)
3. Telecommunications ($11.4M median)

### Highest Settlement Percentages (relative to damages)
1. Healthcare (6.0%)
2. Financial Services (5.3%)
3. Retail (4.6%)

### Industry-Specific Risk Factors

**Pharmaceuticals/Biotech**
- Highest litigation frequency
- Clinical trial-related disclosures
- FDA interactions and approvals
- Product safety and efficacy claims

**Technology**
- AI and emerging technology claims
- Revenue recognition issues
- Product capability misrepresentations
- Cybersecurity and data breaches

**Financial Services**
- Largest defendant asset sizes
- Regulatory compliance issues
- Lending practices
- Investment product disclosures

**Retail/Consumer**
- Supply chain disruptions
- E-commerce transition challenges
- Consumer demand forecasting
- Product quality and safety

**Healthcare Services**
- Billing and reimbursement practices
- Quality of care metrics
- Regulatory compliance
- Acquisition integration issues

**Telecommunications**
- Network performance claims
- Subscriber growth projections
- Technology deployment timelines
- Competitive positioning statements

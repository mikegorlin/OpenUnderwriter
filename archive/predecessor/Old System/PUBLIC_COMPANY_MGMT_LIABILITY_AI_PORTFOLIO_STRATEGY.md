#### Factor 6: AI Washing Risk (10% weight)

**Definition**: Overstating AI capabilities or benefits without substance

| Score | AI Washing Indicators | Risk Level | Red Flags |
|-------|----------------------|------------|-----------|
| **100** | No AI washing - Clear, specific AI use cases | LOW | Detailed AI product descriptions; measurable customer benefits; conservative claims |
| **75** | Minor AI washing - Some marketing hype | LOW-MODERATE | "AI-powered" marketing but real products exist |
| **50** | Moderate AI washing - Vague AI claims | MODERATE | "Leveraging AI" without specifics; rebranding existing products as "AI" |
| **25** | Significant AI washing - Misleading claims | HIGH | "Revolutionary AI" with no product; inflated capability claims; no customer validation |
| **0** | Extreme AI washing - Fraudulent claims | EXTREME | Fake demos; fabricated customer testimonials; no actual AI technology |

**Specific Red Flags**:
- ⚠️ Press releases mentioning "AI" but no AI products in 10-K
- ⚠️ Rebranding existing products as "AI-powered" without technical changes
- ⚠️ Vague AI claims: "leveraging AI," "AI-driven," "AI-enabled" without specifics
- ⚠️ No technical details on AI models, training data, or deployment
- ⚠️ Customer testimonials that don't mention AI benefits
- ⚠️ Sudden "AI pivot" by struggling company (desperation move)
- ⚠️ Board/management with no AI expertise
- 🔴 **EXTREME**: Theranos-style fraud (fake demos, fabricated results)

**Examples**:

| **Struggling company "pivoting to AI"** | VERY HIGH | Desperation move; likely AI washing |
| **Company with vague "AI strategy"** | MODERATE-HIGH | Investigate specifics before binding |

**D&O Underwriting Action**:
- **Score 75-100**: Proceed with standard underwriting
- **Score 50-74**: Enhanced due diligence; require specific AI product details
- **Score 25-49**: Decline or require 100%+ pricing load
- **Score 0-24**: **AUTOMATIC DECLINE** - securities fraud risk

## PART 4: AI WASHING DETECTION FRAMEWORK

### Red Flags Checklist

**Use this checklist for every company claiming AI capabilities**:

| Red Flag | Severity | Investigation Required |
|----------|----------|----------------------|
| ✅ **No AI mentioned in 10-K but press releases tout "AI strategy"** | HIGH | Require specific AI product details; likely AI washing |
| ✅ **Rebranded existing products as "AI-powered" without technical changes** | HIGH | Compare current vs. previous product descriptions; likely AI washing |
| ✅ **Vague AI claims: "leveraging AI," "AI-driven," "AI-enabled"** | MODERATE | Require technical specifics: models, training data, deployment |
| ✅ **No technical details on AI models, algorithms, or infrastructure** | MODERATE | Require CTO/technical leadership interview |
| ✅ **Customer testimonials don't mention AI benefits** | MODERATE | Investigate actual customer experience |
| ✅ **Sudden "AI pivot" by struggling company (revenue declining)** | HIGH | Likely desperation move; AI washing probable |
| ✅ **Board/management with no AI expertise** | MODERATE | Assess technical credibility |
| ✅ **AI revenue not broken out separately in financials** | LOW | May indicate immaterial AI business |
| ✅ **"Revolutionary AI" claims without customer validation** | HIGH | Require proof of customer adoption |
| ✅ **Fake demos or fabricated results** | EXTREME | **AUTOMATIC DECLINE** - securities fraud |

---

### AI Washing Investigation Process

**Step 1: Initial Screen** (10 minutes)
- Review 10-K: Does it mention AI? Specific products/services?
- Review press releases: AI claims vs. 10-K disclosures
- Review website: Specific AI products or vague "AI-powered" claims?

**Step 2: Technical Validation** (30 minutes)
- Identify specific AI products/services
- Assess technical credibility:
  - What AI models are used? (GPT, custom, etc.)
  - What training data?
  - How is AI deployed? (cloud, edge, hybrid)
  - What customer benefits? (measurable productivity gains, cost savings)

**Step 3: Customer Validation** (30 minutes)
- Review customer case studies: Do they mention AI?
- Check customer testimonials: Specific AI benefits?
- Assess customer adoption: How many customers using AI features?

**Step 4: Management Assessment** (30 minutes)
- Review management bios: AI expertise?
- Review board composition: Technical expertise?
- Assess credibility: Conservative claims or hype?

**Step 5: Competitive Comparison** (30 minutes)
- Compare to peers: Is this company's AI real or AI washing?
- Assess differentiation: What makes their AI unique?

**Total Time**: ~2 hours for thorough AI washing assessment

**Decision Matrix**:

| Assessment Result | AI Washing Score | Action |
|------------------|-----------------|--------|
| **Real AI with customer validation** | 75-100 | Proceed with standard underwriting |
| **Real AI but limited adoption** | 50-74 | Enhanced due diligence; require customer traction proof |
| **Vague AI claims, unclear substance** | 25-49 | Decline or require 100%+ pricing load + CEO approval |
| **Fraudulent AI claims (fake demos, fabricated results)** | 0-24 | **AUTOMATIC DECLINE** |

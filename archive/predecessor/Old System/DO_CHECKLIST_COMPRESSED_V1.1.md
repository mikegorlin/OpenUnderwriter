## 📋 FRAMEWORK OVERVIEW

### Purpose
Comprehensive D&O risk assessment for public company underwriting. Systematically evaluates litigation risk, financial health, business model, governance, market dynamics, and alternative data signals.

### Structure
- **PART 1: QUICK SCREEN** (43 checks) - 5-10 minute triage
- **PART 2: COMPREHENSIVE ANALYSIS** (452 checks) - Full analysis

### How to Use
1. Always start with Quick Screen
2. If 3+ red flags → DECLINE immediately
3. If <3 red flags → Proceed to comprehensive analysis
4. Stop early if section stop conditions met
5. Verify all claims with 2+ sources

### Color Coding
- 🟢 GREEN = Pass / Low Risk
- 🟡 YELLOW = Caution / Moderate Risk
- 🔴 RED = Fail / High Risk / Critical
- 🟣 PURPLE = Unknown / Requires Research

## SECTION B: FINANCIAL HEALTH & QUANTITATIVE ANALYSIS (106 checks)

**Purpose**: Deep financial analysis and stock attribution  
**Stop Condition**: Negative OCF + <12mo cash + no revenue OR covenant breach + going concern OR stock down >70% + no recovery

---

### B1: REVENUE QUALITY (15 checks)

**Checks 38-52**: Revenue recognition policy, concentration, growth sustainability, deferred revenue trends, unbilled receivables, contract assets, revenue mix, recurring vs. one-time, geographic concentration, seasonality, customer churn, pricing power, backlog quality, revenue guidance accuracy, revenue per employee

**Key Criteria**:
- 🔴 Revenue concentration >50% one customer | Aggressive recognition | Growth <-20% YoY
- ⚠️ Concentration 25-50% | Moderate concerns | Growth -10% to -20%
- ✅ Diversified <25% | Conservative recognition | Growth >0%

---

### B2: PROFITABILITY ANALYSIS (15 checks)

**Checks 53-67**: Gross margin trends, operating margin, EBITDA margin, net margin, SG&A as % revenue, R&D efficiency, profitability vs. peers, margin expansion/contraction, non-GAAP adjustments, one-time charges frequency, cost structure flexibility, operating leverage, breakeven analysis, path to profitability, burn rate

**Key Criteria**:
- 🔴 Negative gross margin | Operating margin declining >500bps YoY | No path to profitability
- ⚠️ Gross margin <20% | Operating margin declining 200-500bps | Profitability >24mo away
- ✅ Gross margin >40% | Operating margin stable or improving | Profitable or clear path <12mo

---

### B3: BALANCE SHEET HEALTH (15 checks)

**Checks 68-82**: Cash & equivalents, working capital, accounts receivable quality, inventory turnover, accounts payable trends, accrued liabilities, deferred revenue as liability, total debt, net debt, off-balance sheet obligations, lease obligations, pension liabilities, contingent liabilities, asset quality, tangible book value

**Key Criteria**:
- 🔴 Negative working capital | AR days >120 | Inventory obsolete >20% | Net debt >5x EBITDA
- ⚠️ Working capital <$50M | AR days 90-120 | Inventory slow-moving | Net debt 3-5x EBITDA
- ✅ Strong working capital | AR days <60 | Inventory healthy | Net debt <2x EBITDA

---

### B4: CASH FLOW ANALYSIS (15 checks)

**Checks 83-97**: Operating cash flow, free cash flow, cash conversion, capex intensity, working capital changes, cash flow vs. net income, cash flow sustainability, financing cash flow, dividend sustainability, share buyback analysis, cash burn rate, cash runway, cash generation ability, seasonal cash patterns, cash flow guidance

**Key Criteria**:
- 🔴 Negative OCF 3+ quarters | FCF negative + no improvement | Cash burn >$50M/qtr + <6mo runway
- ⚠️ OCF declining >30% YoY | FCF breakeven | Cash burn concerning but >12mo runway
- ✅ OCF positive & growing | FCF positive | Self-sustaining or >24mo runway

---

### B5: ACCOUNTING QUALITY (12 checks)

**Checks 98-109**: Auditor quality, audit fees trends, non-audit fees, auditor tenure, restatement history, SEC comment letters, aggressive accounting policies, reserves adequacy, accruals quality, earnings quality score, days sales outstanding, audit committee effectiveness

**Key Criteria**:
- 🔴 Non-Big 4 auditor + going concern | Restatement <24mo | SEC comment unresolved | High accruals
- ⚠️ Big 4 but high non-audit fees | Minor restatement >24mo | SEC comments resolved | Moderate accruals
- ✅ Big 4 auditor, low non-audit fees | No restatements | No SEC issues | Conservative accounting

---

### B6: DEBT & COVENANTS (8 checks)

**Checks 110-117**: Total debt levels, debt maturity schedule, covenant compliance, refinancing risk, interest coverage ratio, debt service coverage, credit ratings, debt structure

**Key Criteria**:
- 🔴 Covenant breach | Debt matures <12mo + no refinancing | Interest coverage <1.0x | CCC rating
- ⚠️ Near covenant limits | Debt matures 12-24mo | Interest coverage 1.0-2.0x | B rating
- ✅ Covenant cushion >20% | Debt matures >24mo or refinanced | Interest coverage >3.0x | BB+ or better

---

### B7: STOCK PERFORMANCE ATTRIBUTION (20 checks)

**Checks 118-137**: 52-week high/low, YTD return, 1-year return, 3-year return, stock vs. sector, stock vs. peers, volatility analysis, beta, correlation to sector, single-day drops >10%, event attribution, recovery patterns, support/resistance levels, technical indicators, volume analysis, bid-ask spread, block trades, dark pool activity, short interest trends, options activity

**Key Criteria**:
- 🔴 Stock down >50% + company-specific trigger + no recovery | Multiple drops >20% + fraud allegations
- ⚠️ Stock down 30-50% + sector-wide pressure | Volatility high but recovering
- ✅ Stock down <30% or outperforming sector | Volatility normal | No concerning patterns

**Attribution Requirement**: For ANY decline >20%, MUST identify:
- Specific trigger event with date
- Stock reaction magnitude
- Peer/sector comparison
- Attribution (company-specific vs. sector vs. macro)

---

### B8: INDUSTRY OPERATING METRICS (6 checks)

**Checks 138-143**: Industry-specific KPIs based on sector:
- **SaaS**: ARR growth, net revenue retention, CAC payback, LTV/CAC, churn rate, magic number
- **Retail**: Same-store sales, inventory turnover, sales per sq ft
- **Life Sciences**: Cash runway, clinical trial progress, regulatory milestones
- **Financial Services**: Net interest margin, efficiency ratio, NPL ratio
- **Energy**: Production costs, reserves replacement, finding & development costs
- **Manufacturing**: Capacity utilization, order backlog, inventory days

**Key Criteria**: Compare to industry benchmarks, flag if bottom quartile

### SECTION B DECISION POINT

**Stop if ANY**:

# QUICK SCREEN: CRITICAL RED FLAG CHECKLIST

**Purpose**: Rapidly identify auto-decline triggers before running full 6-module analysis

**Time Required**: 5-10 minutes

**Usage**: Run this Quick Screen FIRST. If 3+ auto-decline triggers found, STOP and recommend DECLINE without full analysis.

**Total Checks**: 40 critical red flags across all 6 modules

---

## DECISION LOGIC

**If 3+ RED FLAGS below → IMMEDIATE DECLINE (don't waste time on full analysis)**

**If 1-2 RED FLAGS → Proceed to full 6-module analysis with HIGH RISK flag**

**If 0 RED FLAGS → Proceed to full 6-module analysis**

---

## LITIGATION & LEGAL (Module 1) - 8 Checks

### ❗ QS-1.1: Active Securities Class Action
- **Check**: Active securities fraud class action lawsuit
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + settlement >$20M OR recent filing (<12 months)
- **Sources**: Stanford Securities Clearinghouse, PACER, Form 8-K

### ❗ QS-1.2: SEC Enforcement Investigation  
- **Check**: Formal SEC investigation disclosed
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + accounting/fraud allegations
- **Sources**: Form 8-K Item 1.01, 8.01, press releases

### ❗ QS-1.3: DOJ Criminal Investigation
- **Check**: Department of Justice criminal investigation
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + active investigation
- **Sources**: Form 8-K, news reports

### ❗ QS-1.4: Financial Restatement
- **Check**: Financial restatement announced in past 24 months
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + material restatement (revenue or earnings)
- **Sources**: Form 8-K Item 4.02, amended 10-K/10-Q

### ❗ QS-1.5: Going Concern Warning
- **Check**: Auditor issued going concern opinion
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes (current or past 12 months)
- **Sources**: 10-K audit opinion

### ❗ QS-1.6: Auditor Resignation
- **Check**: Auditor resigned in past 24 months
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + resignation due to disagreement or fraud concerns
- **Sources**: Form 8-K Item 4.01

### ❗ QS-1.7: Bankruptcy Filing
- **Check**: Company filed for bankruptcy protection
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes (Chapter 11 or Chapter 7)
- **Sources**: PACER, news

### ❗ QS-1.8: Delisting Notice
- **Check**: Exchange delisting notice or warning
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + failure to cure deficiency
- **Sources**: Form 8-K, exchange notices

## FINANCIAL DISTRESS (Module 2) - 8 Checks

### ❗ QS-2.1: Negative Cash Position
- **Check**: Cash + equivalents AND operating cash flow
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Cash <$10M + negative operating cash flow + <12 months runway
- **Sources**: Balance sheet, cash flow statement

### ❗ QS-2.2: Debt Covenant Breach
- **Check**: Debt covenant violation or waiver
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + going concern OR refinancing failure
- **Sources**: Debt note in 10-K, Form 8-K

### ❗ QS-2.3: Interest Coverage Crisis
- **Check**: EBIT / Interest expense
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: <1.5x (can't cover interest)
- **Sources**: Income statement

### ❗ QS-2.4: Revenue Collapse
- **Check**: Revenue decline year-over-year
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Revenue down >40% YoY
- **Sources**: Income statement, 10-Q

### ❗ QS-2.5: Stock Price Catastrophe
- **Check**: Stock decline from 52-week high
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Down >70% from peak
- **Sources**: Stock price data

### ❗ QS-2.6: Credit Rating Collapse
- **Check**: S&P/Moody's/Fitch credit rating
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: CCC+ or lower (distressed)
- **Sources**: Rating agency reports

### ❗ QS-2.7: Accounting Quality Red Flag
- **Check**: Audit Integrity AGR score (if available)
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: AGR <40 ("Very Aggressive") + other red flags
- **Sources**: Audit Integrity, Bloomberg

### ❗ QS-2.8: Material Weakness in Controls
- **Check**: Material weakness in internal controls
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + accounting issues or restatement
- **Sources**: 10-K Item 9A

## BUSINESS MODEL COLLAPSE (Module 3) - 6 Checks

### ❗ QS-3.1: Core Product Failure (Life Sciences)
- **Check**: FDA Complete Response Letter (drug rejection)
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + no alternative products + revenue declining >50%
- **Sources**: Form 8-K, FDA website

### ❗ QS-3.2: Major Customer Loss
- **Check**: Lost customer representing >50% of revenue
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + no replacement + guidance cut >40%
- **Sources**: Customer disclosures, revenue guidance

### ❗ QS-3.3: Failed M&A Integration
- **Check**: Major acquisition + goodwill impairment
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Acquisition >50% of market cap + impairment >30% + customer losses >25%
- **Sources**: Acquisition disclosures, goodwill note

### ❗ QS-3.4: Market Share Collapse
- **Check**: Market share decline
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Share down >30% over 2 years + no competitive response
- **Sources**: Industry reports, company disclosures

### ❗ QS-3.5: Product Safety Crisis
- **Check**: Major product recall or safety issue
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Deaths/serious injuries + regulatory action + class action
- **Sources**: FDA recalls, CPSC, news

### ❗ QS-3.6: Supply Chain Collapse
- **Check**: Single-source supplier failure
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Critical supplier failed + no alternative + production halted >60 days
- **Sources**: Supply chain disclosures, news

## GOVERNANCE CRISIS (Module 4) - 6 Checks

### ❗ QS-4.1: CEO + CFO Simultaneous Departures
- **Check**: CEO and CFO both departed within 6 months
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + accounting issues + insider selling before departure
- **Sources**: Form 8-K Item 5.02, Form 4

### ❗ QS-4.2: Board Independence Failure
- **Check**: Board independence <50%
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + related party transactions + weak audit committee
- **Sources**: Proxy statement (DEF 14A)

### ❗ QS-4.3: Insider Trading Violations
- **Check**: SEC enforcement for insider trading
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Yes + criminal charges
- **Sources**: SEC enforcement releases, news

### ❗ QS-4.4: Failed Say-on-Pay Vote
- **Check**: Shareholder rejection of executive compensation
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: <50% approval + excessive pay + poor performance
- **Sources**: Proxy statement, Form 8-K

### ❗ QS-4.5: Insider Selling Before Bad News
- **Check**: Heavy insider selling 30-90 days before material negative event
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: 3+ insiders selling >30% of holdings + stock drop >30% on bad news
- **Sources**: Form 4, event dates, stock price

### ❗ QS-4.6: Active Proxy Contest
- **Check**: Activist investor proxy contest
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Active contest + management entrenchment + stock down >50%
- **Sources**: Schedule 13D, proxy materials

## MARKET COLLAPSE (Module 5) - 6 Checks

### ❗ QS-5.1: Credible Short Seller Fraud Report
- **Check**: Short seller report alleging fraud
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Muddy Waters/Hindenburg report + stock down >50% + no recovery + SEC investigation
- **Sources**: Short seller websites, stock price, Form 8-K

### ❗ QS-5.2: Analyst Abandonment
- **Check**: Sell-side analyst coverage
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: All analysts dropped coverage OR all downgraded to Sell + price targets cut >60%
- **Sources**: Bloomberg, analyst reports

- **Check**: Institutional ownership decline
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Institutional ownership down >25% in 2 quarters + top 10 holders all reducing
- **Sources**: 13F filings

### ❗ QS-5.4: Extreme Short Interest
- **Check**: Short interest as % of float
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: >30% short interest + increasing + stock declining
- **Sources**: Exchange short interest data

### ❗ QS-5.5: Geopolitical Operations Halt
- **Check**: Operations halted due to geopolitical event
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: >70% revenue from conflict region + operations halted + stock down >60%
- **Sources**: Geographic revenue, news, Form 8-K

### ❗ QS-5.6: Cybersecurity Catastrophe
- **Check**: Major data breach
- **🔴 AUTO-DECLINE**: Major breach + customer data compromised + regulatory fines + class action + stock down >40%
- **Sources**: Form 8-K, news, breach databases

## NOTES ON QUICK SCREEN

### Purpose
The Quick Screen is designed to **save time** by identifying obvious auto-decline cases before investing 3-5 hours in full analysis.

### Limitations
- Quick Screen may miss "soft" red flags that emerge in full analysis
- Some companies with 0-2 Quick Screen red flags will still be DECLINE after full analysis
- Quick Screen is **not a substitute** for full analysis on borderline cases

### When to Skip Quick Screen
- **Never skip Quick Screen** - it only takes 10 minutes and can save hours

### Calibration
- If you find yourself declining >50% of companies that pass Quick Screen, consider adding more Quick Screen checks
- If you rarely decline companies that pass Quick Screen, Quick Screen may be too strict

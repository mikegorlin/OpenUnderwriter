## RECONCILING CONFLICTING DATA

### **Customer Concentration Discrepancy**:
- **Fitch (Dec 10)**: Microsoft = 67% of Q3 2025 revenue
- **Seeking Alpha (Dec 12)**: OpenAI = 70% of revenue

**Likely Explanation**: 
- Fitch is citing Q3 2025 specifically (Microsoft 67%)
- Seeking Alpha may be citing different time period or forward-looking contracts
- Both sources agree on high customer concentration (>60%)

### **Stock Decline Percentage**:
- **WSJ**: 46% decline in 6 weeks
- **Forbes**: 62% decline from peak
- **CNBC/Economic Times/CoinDesk**: 60-61% decline from June peak

**Likely Explanation**:
- WSJ (Dec 15): 46% decline from recent peak (likely November peak)
- Forbes/CNBC/Economic Times (Dec 16): 60-62% decline from all-time high (June 2025 peak of $187)
- Both are correct, just different reference points

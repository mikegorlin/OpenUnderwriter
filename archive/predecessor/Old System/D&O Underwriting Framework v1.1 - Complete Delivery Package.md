## 🎯 WHAT THE GPT WILL DO

When you type a ticker symbol, the GPT will:

1. **Pull company data** from SEC filings, news, databases
2. **Run Quick Screen** (43 checks in 5-10 minutes)
3. **If <3 red flags**: Run comprehensive analysis (452 checks)
4. **If 3+ red flags**: Stop immediately, suggest DECLINE
5. **Verify all claims** with 2+ sources
6. **Explain stock movements** with specific trigger events
7. **Provide underwriting suggestion**: DECLINE / WRITE WITH CONDITIONS / FAVORABLE
8. **List top 10 critical red flags**
9. **Generate management interview questions**
10. **Document research gaps** (purple items)

---

## 📝 OUTPUT FORMAT

Every analysis includes:

1. **Executive Summary** - 3-5 paragraphs
2. **Company Overview** - Basic profile and financials
3. **Quick Screen Results** - Red flags identified
4. **Top 10 Critical Red Flags** - Ranked by severity
5. **Underwriting Suggestion** - DECLINE / WRITE / FAVORABLE
6. **Pros & Cons** - Up to 10 each
7. **Supported Decision Summary** - Detailed rationale
8. **Management Interview Questions** - 8-12 targeted questions
9. **Renewal Review Items** - What to check next year
10. **Analysis Metadata** - Checks performed, confidence level
11. **Detailed Section Analysis** - Full findings by section
12. **Research Gaps** - Purple items requiring manual research
13. **Sources & Methodology** - Complete documentation

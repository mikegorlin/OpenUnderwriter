# Cassava Sciences SEC Charges - Key Facts

**Date**: September 26, 2024

**Settlement Amounts**:
- Cassava Sciences: $40 million
- Remi Barbier (Founder/Former CEO): $175,000 + 3-year officer-and-director bar
- Dr. Lindsay Burns (Former SVP Neuroscience): $85,000 + 5-year officer-and-director bar
- Dr. Hoau-Yan Wang (Consultant/CUNY Professor): $50,000

**Total Settlement**: $40,310,000

**Key Allegations**:

1. **Data Manipulation**: Dr. Wang received information that unblinded him to Phase 2 clinical data, allowing him to identify ~33% of patients. He manipulated data to create appearance of dramatic improvements in biomarkers (total tau, phosphorylated tau).

2. **Misleading Blinding Claims**: Cassava and Burns misled investors by claiming Phase 2 trial was conducted in blinded conditions, despite Wang being unblinded.

3. **Cherry-Picked Cognitive Data**: Cassava claimed significant improvement in episodic memory but failed to disclose that full patient dataset showed NO measurable cognitive improvement - only hand-selected subset showed improvement.

4. **Undisclosed Conflicts**: Cassava and Barbier failed to disclose Wang's role despite his personal, financial, and professional interest in therapeutic's success.

**Charges**:
- Antifraud violations (Securities Act)
- Reporting violations (Exchange Act)
- Aiding and abetting violations (Wang)

**Outcome**: Without admitting or denying allegations, all parties settled.

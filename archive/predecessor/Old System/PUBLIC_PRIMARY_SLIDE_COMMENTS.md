### SLIDE 11: Distinctions – in Loss paid between Side-A/B
**Content**: Comparison table showing how Liberty, Chubb, and AIG define Side A vs. Side B coverage

| Insuring Agreement | Liberty | Chubb | AIG |
|-------------------|---------|-------|-----|
| **Side-A** | to the extent such Loss is **Non-Indemnifiable** | except to the extent such Loss has been paid or indemnified | unless an Organization has paid or indemnified such Loss |
| **Side-B** | to the extent the **Insured Entity** is required or permitted to indemnify such Insured Person for such Loss | to the extent the **Organization** pays or indemnifies such Loss | To the extent that such **Organization** has indemnified such Loss of, or paid such Loss on behalf of, such Insured Person |

**Non-Indemnifiable means Loss:**
- (A) incurred by an Insured Person; and

## **Final Version - Red Flags First, EPL Detailed**

**Date**: December 2025  
**Purpose**: Practical framework for underwriters - check red flags FIRST, then assess optionality/financial/execution

---

## THE CORE QUESTION

> **"Does this company survive if AI fails?"**

**If YES** → Consider underwriting  
**If NO** → Decline

---

## FRAMEWORK STRUCTURE

**Check in This Order**:

1. **LENS 1: Red Flags** (CHECK FIRST - Disqualifying)
   - If ANY present → **DECLINE immediately**
   - Don't waste time on other lenses

2. **LENS 2: Optionality** (40% weight)
   - Do they survive if AI fails?

3. **LENS 3: Financial Health** (30% weight)
   - Can they afford AI from operating cash flow?

4. **LENS 4: Execution Capability** (20% weight)
   - Can they deliver on AI strategy?

5. **SEPARATE: EPL Risk Assessment** (10% weight)
   - Not disqualifying, but affects pricing/terms
   - Green/Yellow/Red classification

## LENS 1: RED FLAGS (CHECK FIRST)

### **The Question**: "Are there any automatic deal-breakers?"

### **7 Automatic DECLINE Triggers** (Any ONE = Decline):

#### **1. Circular Deals >20% of Revenue**

**What It Is**: Company A invests in Company B → Company B purchases services from Company A

**Why It's Disqualifying**: Enron parallel - circular deals inflate revenue → Fraud → Bankruptcy → $7B+ D&O

**Examples**:
- ❌ **Oracle/OpenAI**: Oracle building $300B data centers primarily for OpenAI (40% of AI capex) → **DECLINE**
- ⚠️ **Microsoft/OpenAI**: Microsoft invests $13B → OpenAI commits $300B cloud spend (10-15% circular) → **MONITOR**
- ✅ **Salesforce**: No circular deals → **CLEAN**

**Threshold**: >20% = AUTOMATIC DECLINE

---

#### **2. Single Customer Concentration >40%**

**What It Is**: One customer represents >40% of revenue, capex, or strategic importance

**Why It's Disqualifying**: Existential risk if customer leaves → Stock collapse → Massive D&O exposure

**Examples**:
- ❌ **Oracle**: OpenAI ~40% of AI capex → **DECLINE**
- ❌ **Palantir**: US Government ~40% of revenue → **DECLINE**
- ✅ **Microsoft**: No customer >5% → **CLEAN**

**Threshold**: >40% = AUTOMATIC DECLINE

---

#### **3. AI Washing / Fraudulent AI Claims**

**What It Is**: Company overstates AI capabilities, makes false AI claims, or rebrands without substance

**Why It's Disqualifying**: Theranos parallel - overstated technology → Securities fraud → Criminal charges

**Examples**:
- ❌ **SEC enforcement cases**: Delphia, Global Predictions charged with false AI claims → **DECLINE**
- ⚠️ **Traditional companies suddenly "AI companies"**: Vague claims, no technical substance → **SCRUTINIZE**
- ✅ **Microsoft, Salesforce**: Real AI products, real revenue, transparent → **CLEAN**

**Red Flags**:
- Vague "AI-powered" claims without specifics
- Sudden rebranding as "AI company"
- No patents, no papers, no technical details
- AI claims in marketing ≠ AI disclosures in 10-K
- No AI revenue despite "AI company" claims

**Threshold**: Fraudulent claims = AUTOMATIC DECLINE

---

#### **4. Extreme Valuation (>100x Revenue for Pre-Revenue)**

**What It Is**: Company valuation disconnected from fundamentals (dot-com bubble territory)

**Why It's Disqualifying**: When bubble bursts → Stock collapses 80-100% → Massive securities litigation

**Examples**:
- ❌ **Pets.com (2000)**: $300M valuation, $619K revenue = 485x → Bankruptcy in 9 months
- ⚠️ **Pre-revenue AI startups**: $1B+ valuations, no revenue → **BUBBLE TERRITORY**
- ✅ **Microsoft**: ~12x revenue → **REASONABLE**

**Threshold**: >100x revenue for pre-revenue companies = AUTOMATIC DECLINE

---

#### **5. Fraud History / SEC Enforcement**

**What It Is**: Company has history of fraud, restatements, or ongoing SEC enforcement

**Why It's Disqualifying**: Recidivist risk - companies with fraud history have higher probability of future fraud

**Examples**:
- ❌ **Wells Fargo**: Fake accounts scandal → Multiple subsequent scandals → **DECLINE**
- ❌ **Tesla**: Multiple D&O lawsuits (compensation, SolarCity, going-private) → **DECLINE**
- ✅ **Microsoft, Salesforce**: Clean history → **CLEAN**

**Red Flags**:
- Financial restatements (especially multiple)
- SEC enforcement actions
- DOJ criminal investigations
- Accounting fraud
- Rapid CFO/auditor turnover

**Threshold**: Fraud history or ongoing enforcement = AUTOMATIC DECLINE

---

#### **6. Product Liability - Death/Serious Harm**

**What It Is**: AI product causes or contributes to death, serious harm, or exploitation of vulnerable users (especially minors)

**Why It's Disqualifying**: Tobacco/opioid parallel - products causing death = massive liability ($206B tobacco, $50B+ opioids)

**Examples**:
- ❌ **Character.AI**: 2 teen suicides, 1 self-harm, 1 sexual abuse → Federal lawsuit allowed to proceed → Section 230 defense REJECTED → **AUTOMATIC DECLINE**
- ❌ **Replika**: Romantic AI companion; reports of inappropriate content to minors → **DECLINE**
- ✅ **Microsoft Copilot**: Enterprise/adults, robust safety controls, no incidents → **CLEAN**

**Red Flags**:
- AI product marketed to minors without safety controls
- AI companions creating emotional bonds with vulnerable users
- No content filtering (self-harm, violence, sexual)
- No distress detection systems
- No warnings about psychological risks
- Prior incidents (death, serious harm, exploitation)

**Threshold**: Death, serious harm, or exploitation of minors = AUTOMATIC DECLINE

---

#### **7. Mass AI-Driven Layoffs (>30% Workforce)**

**What It Is**: Company planning >30% workforce reduction due to AI automation

**Why It's Disqualifying**: Class action EPL risk - "AI replaced me" narrative → Sympathetic plaintiffs → Massive EPL exposure

**Note**: This is the EXTREME threshold for automatic decline. Layoffs <30% are assessed in separate EPL section below.

**Examples**:
- ❌ **Companies announcing >30% AI automation layoffs** → **AUTOMATIC DECLINE**
- ⚠️ **IBM**: Ongoing age discrimination claims from AI-driven reductions → **EPL SURCHARGE** (see EPL section)
- ✅ **Microsoft, Salesforce**: Minimal layoffs (<5%) → **CLEAN**

**Threshold**: >30% workforce reduction = AUTOMATIC DECLINE

### **Red Flag Assessment**:

| Red Flags Present? | Action |
|-------------------|--------|
| **ANY of the 7 triggers** | 🔴 **AUTOMATIC DECLINE** - Stop assessment here |
| **None** | ✅ Proceed to Lens 2 (Optionality) |

**DO NOT proceed to other lenses if ANY red flag is present.**

# SECTION 1: ANALYSIS INSTRUCTIONS

## CATEGORY A: LITIGATION & REGULATORY RISK (~20 checks)

### A.1: COMPANY PROFILE & CLASSIFICATION

**A.1.1: Basic Company Information**
- **Risk Area**: Foundational company data for risk assessment
- **Sources**: 10-K, company website, Yahoo Finance
- **Assess**: Company name, ticker, industry/sector, headquarters, fiscal year end, market cap, public company tenure
- **Severity**: 🟣 UNKNOWN if key data missing | ✅ LOW if complete profile obtained

**A.1.2: Market Capitalization & Life Stage**
- **Risk Area**: Company size and maturity affecting D&O risk profile
- **Sources**: Yahoo Finance, 10-K financial history
- **Assess**: Current market cap, market cap tier (mega/large/mid/small/micro), life stage (mature/growth/pre-revenue/distressed), years profitable
- **Severity**:
  - 🔴 CRITICAL: Micro-cap (<$300M) + pre-revenue + distressed
  - 🔴 HIGH: Small-cap + pre-revenue OR distressed
  - ⚠️ MODERATE: Mid-cap + growth stage
  - ✅ LOW: Large/mega-cap + mature + profitable 5+ years

**A.1.3: SPAC Status & Performance**
- **Risk Area**: De-SPAC companies with elevated litigation risk
- **Sources**: 8-K, merger documents, stock performance
- **Assess**: SPAC merger date, projections vs. actuals, stock performance post-merger, litigation filed
- **Severity**:
  - 🔴 CRITICAL: De-SPAC <24mo + projections missed >50% + stock down >60% + litigation filed
  - 🔴 HIGH: De-SPAC <24mo + projections missed 30-50% + stock down 40-60%
  - ⚠️ MODERATE: De-SPAC <24mo + projections missed <30%
  - ✅ LOW: Not a SPAC OR de-SPAC >24mo + met projections

### A.2: SECURITIES LITIGATION

**A.2.1: Securities Class Action Lawsuits**
- **Risk Area**: Active or recent securities fraud litigation
- **Sources**: Stanford Securities Clearinghouse, 8-K, 10-K/10-Q Legal Proceedings, PACER
- **Assess**: Active lawsuits, dismissed <24mo, class period, allegations (10b-5, Section 11/12), estimated settlement, parallel derivative suits
- **Severity**:
  - 🔴 CRITICAL: Active lawsuit + estimated settlement >$50M OR >10% market cap
  - 🔴 HIGH: Active lawsuit + settlement $10-50M OR multiple active lawsuits (2+) OR accounting fraud allegations
  - ⚠️ MODERATE: Lawsuit dismissed <12mo (appeal risk) OR settlement <$10M
  - ✅ LOW: No securities litigation in 5+ years

**A.2.2: Derivative Lawsuits**
- **Risk Area**: Shareholder derivative suits alleging breach of fiduciary duty
- **Sources**: Delaware Court of Chancery, PACER, company disclosures
- **Assess**: Active derivative suits, Caremark duty of oversight claims, demand refused/excused, parallel securities action
- **Severity**:
  - 🔴 CRITICAL: 2+ active derivative suits + Caremark claims + parallel securities action
  - 🔴 HIGH: 1 active derivative suit + Caremark claims OR demand refused
  - ⚠️ MODERATE: Derivative suit dismissed <12mo
  - ✅ LOW: No derivative litigation in 5+ years

### A.3: REGULATORY ENFORCEMENT

**A.3.1: SEC Enforcement Actions**
- **Risk Area**: SEC civil enforcement for accounting/disclosure violations
- **Sources**: SEC.gov Litigation Releases, 8-K, company disclosures
- **Assess**: Active enforcement, settlement <24mo, violations alleged, penalties, officer/director bars, disgorgement
- **Severity**:
  - 🔴 CRITICAL: Active enforcement OR settlement <12mo + penalties >$25M OR officer bars
  - 🔴 HIGH: Settlement 12-24mo + penalties $5-25M OR disgorgement required
  - ⚠️ MODERATE: Settlement >24mo + penalties <$5M
  - ✅ LOW: No SEC enforcement in 5+ years

**A.3.2: DOJ Criminal Investigations**
- **Risk Area**: Criminal charges against company or executives
- **Sources**: DOJ press releases, 8-K, news, court records
- **Assess**: Criminal investigation disclosed, charges filed, target letters, plea agreements, deferred prosecution agreements (DPA)
- **Severity**:
  - 🔴 CRITICAL: Criminal charges filed against company or executives
  - 🔴 HIGH: Target letter disclosed OR investigation disclosed + subpoenas issued
  - ⚠️ MODERATE: DPA or NPA entered <36mo
  - ✅ LOW: No criminal investigations disclosed

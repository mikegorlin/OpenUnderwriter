# MODULE 6: ALTERNATIVE DATA & BLIND SPOT ANALYSIS

**Purpose**: Capture early warning signals from non-traditional sources including social media, employee reviews, customer feedback, news patterns, scientific community, regulatory databases, and competitive intelligence that often predict D&O litigation before it appears in SEC filings.

**Total Checks**: ~90 enumerated checks across 10 categories

**When to Use**:
- Run after Module 5 or in parallel with other modules
- Critical for identifying "soft signals" and emerging risks
- Often reveals problems 3-12 months before they appear in financial statements
- **Verification Standard**: Require 2+ independent sources for all soft signals

**Auto-Decline Triggers in This Module**:
- Viral fraud allegations (>1M views) + employee whistleblower corroboration + investigative journalism + no credible rebuttal
- Mass employee exodus (>30% turnover) + Glassdoor rating <2.5 + fraud allegations in reviews + executive departures
- Product safety crisis (deaths/injuries) + viral social media + regulatory action + customer lawsuits
- Investigative journalism exposé (WSJ, Bloomberg, NYT) + fraud/misconduct allegations + company silence + stock down >40%

## VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS FOR MODULE 6

**Critical**: Module 6 relies on "soft signals" that can be unreliable. **REQUIRE 2+ INDEPENDENT SOURCES** before flagging as red.

### Verification Standard by Source Type:

**Social Media (Twitter/X, Reddit, TikTok)**:
- **Minimum**: 2+ independent sources (e.g., Twitter + Reddit)
- **Preferred**: 3+ sources (social media + news + employee reviews)
- **Red Flag Threshold**: Viral content (>100K engagement) + corroboration

**Employee Reviews (Glassdoor, Blind, Indeed)**:
- **Minimum**: 3+ reviews with similar allegations
- **Preferred**: 5+ reviews + corroboration from news or departures
- **Red Flag Threshold**: Specific fraud allegations in multiple reviews

**Customer Reviews (Amazon, App Store, BBB)**:
- **Minimum**: Pattern of similar complaints (10+ reviews)
- **Preferred**: Complaints + regulatory database evidence (FDA, CPSC)
- **Red Flag Threshold**: Safety complaints + regulatory action

**News & Journalism**:
- **Minimum**: 1 credible source (WSJ, Bloomberg, NYT, Reuters)
- **Preferred**: 2+ outlets reporting same story
- **Red Flag Threshold**: Investigative report from tier-1 outlet

**Regulatory Databases (FDA, EPA, OSHA)**:
- **Minimum**: 1 authoritative source (government database)
- **Verification**: Cross-check with company disclosures
- **Red Flag Threshold**: Enforcement action disclosed

**Scientific Community (PubPeer, Retraction Watch)**:
- **Minimum**: 2+ credible scientists raising concerns
- **Preferred**: Multiple scientists + journal investigation
- **Red Flag Threshold**: Retraction for fraud or multiple scientists with institutional affiliations

## Page 2: DEAL CONTEXT & COMPANY

### 3. DEAL CONTEXT
- Layer, premium, rate change, expiring vs proposed, why available, relationship value, full tower with carriers/limits/premiums, broker intel
- STM Calculation
- ART Snapshot

### 4. COMPANY & BUSINESS
- **Overview**: What they do? Key operations? How do they make money?
- **Event Status**: IPO/SPAC timing, major M&A, recent capital raises — anything that elevates litigation risk
- **Concentration**: Customer, supplier, geographic, product — flag HIGH/MOD/LOW with percentages
- **Industry Position**: Market position, share trend, headwinds, tailwinds
- **Industry-Specific**: Tariffs, unions, regulatory, sector KPIs
- **Macro Exposure**: Interest rate sensitivity, currency exposure, commodity dependence, recession sensitivity
- **Technology Dependence**: Critical platform/IP risks, single system dependencies, cybersecurity posture
- **Restructuring**: Layoffs, closures, cost programs

## Page 3: STOCK & MARKET

### 5. STOCK & MARKET
- **Price & Performance**: Current price, 52-week high/low, decline from high, performance vs sector ETF over 1/2/5 years
- **Ownership & Insider Activity**: Institutional %, insider %, top holders, insider sales with $ amounts and 10b5-1 status
- **Institutional Ownership Changes**: Increasing or decreasing? Major exits?
- **Stock Drops >10%**: Date, magnitude, trigger, sector comparison, attribution, litigation status
- **Short Interest**: % of float, trend, days to cover, any short seller reports
- **Analyst Sentiment**: Coverage count, consensus, price target, recent changes

### 6. FINANCIAL CONDITION
- **Results**: Revenue, income, margins, profitability status, trend
- **Liquidity & Leverage**: Cash, revolver, total liquidity, runway if burning, debt, Debt/EBITDA, covenants, upcoming maturities
- **Going Concern**: Auditor language, management response
- **Guidance Track Record**: Misses in past 8 quarters with stock impact, average miss magnitude
- **Accounting Quality**: Revenue recognition complexity, A/R growth vs revenue, inventory buildup, goodwill impairment risk
- **Outlook**: Management guidance summary

### 7. GOVERNANCE & MANAGEMENT
- **Leadership**: CEO name/tenure/background, CFO name/tenure/qualifications
- **Background Issues**: Prior litigation, company failures, credibility issues
- **Recent Changes**: Executive or board departures/appointments in past 12 months
- **Board**: Total directors, independence %, CEO=Chair?, lead independent?
- **Ownership Structure**: Dual-class? Activist involvement?
- **Compensation & Conflicts**: Short-term stock incentives, failed say-on-pay (<70% approval), related party transactions

## Page 4: LITIGATION & FORWARD LOOK

### 8. LITIGATION & CLAIMS
- **Securities Litigation**: Stanford SCAC search date, cases found with status/class period/allegations
- **Other Litigation**: Derivative, SEC, DOJ, regulatory matters
- **Industry & Competitor Litigation**: Peers sued for similar practices, sector-wide enforcement trends
- **Internal Issues**: Whistleblower complaints, board-level investigations, SEC comment letters
- **Accounting Issues**: Restatements, auditor changes, material weaknesses
- **Claims on Liberty Policy**: Matter description, filed date, layer, factual / disclosed info

### 9. FORWARD LOOK
- **Prospective Triggers**: Upcoming events that could cause stock drops and litigation
- **News & Media**: Recent coverage, investigative reports
- **Alternative Signals**: Glassdoor rating/trend, customer reviews, LinkedIn headcount changes, job posting patterns, regulatory database flags, social media sentiment

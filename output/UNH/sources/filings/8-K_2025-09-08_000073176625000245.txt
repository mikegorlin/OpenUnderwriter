Regulation FD Disclosure. Senior leaders from UnitedHealth Group Incorporated (the "Company") will be meeting with investors and analysts between September 8, 2025 and September 10, 2025, during which the Company intends to reaffirm our adjusted 2025 earnings per share expectations previously disclosed by the Company on July 29, 2025. This outlook incorporates closing the acquisition of Amedisys in August 2025, which is expected to be modestly dilutive to adjusted earnings per share due to financing costs and the integration related investments.

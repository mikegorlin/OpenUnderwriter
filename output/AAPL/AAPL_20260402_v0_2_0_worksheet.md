## Section 3: Financial Health -- Capitalization, Cash Generation & Distress Indicators

However, the elevated debt-to-equity of 1.23, against an exceptionally high interest coverage ratio of 33.8, reflects aggressive capital returns; a sustained market downturn could fuel derivative suits alleging fiduciary breach in capital allocation. The Altman Z-Score of 10.16 confirms fundamental safety in the "safe" zone, but the real D&O exposure lies in event-driven litigation targeting its strategic decisions, not its balance sheet, as underscored by a clean audit from Ernst & Young after a 17-year tenure.

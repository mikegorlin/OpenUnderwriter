On June 9, 2025, the Board of Directors of The Procter & Gamble Company (the "Company") appointed Craig Arnold, former Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Eaton Corporation, as a Director of the Company, effective June 9. Mr. Arnold will serve on the Audit and Compensation & Leadership Development Committees. The Board has determined that Mr. Arnold is independent under the NYSE listing standards and the Company's Independence Guidelines. Mr. Arnold will receive the same compensation as the other non-employee members of the Board of Directors, including a pro-rated annual RSU grant that reflects his June appointment date. The Company's Director compensation program is described in the Company's definitive proxy statement dated August 23, 2024. The Company is filing the information under this item pursuant to Item 5.02(d), "Election of Directors."

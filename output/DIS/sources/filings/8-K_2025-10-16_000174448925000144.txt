On October 15, 2025, The Walt Disney Company (the "Company") and Kristina K. Schake, the Company's Senior Executive Vice President and Chief Communications Officer, entered into an amendment (the "Amendment") to Ms. Schake's employment agreement. Pursuant to the Amendment, the term of Ms. Schake's employment agreement was extended to June 30, 2027. The Amendment also increased Ms. Schake's annual base salary to $875,000, effective on October 15, 2025, with future increases at the Company's discretion. Ms. Schake's target annual bonus opportunity and target long-term equity incentive annual award value, in each case, as a percentage of her base salary were unchanged. A copy of the Amendment is attached as Exhibit 10.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and incorporated by reference herein.

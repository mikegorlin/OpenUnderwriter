At the meeting, the matters set forth below were submitted for a vote of security holders. As of the close of business on April 2, 2025, which was the record date for the Annual Meeting, 4,308,968,407 shares of ExxonMobil common stock, without par value ("Common Stock"), were outstanding and entitled to vote.

# WTW 2025 Life Sciences Litigation Trends (Oct 30, 2025)

## Key Statistics from WTW Report

### SCA Filing Data
- SCA filings against life science companies increased 15% in 2024 over 2023
- Total of 50 cases in 2024, representing 22% of all SCA filings
- Overall SCA filings: 225 in 2024 vs 212 in 2023

### Complaint Allegation Breakdown
- 52% involved alleged misrepresentations regarding product efficacy and safety (FDA approval impact)
- 34% arose from alleged misrepresentations involving regulatory hurdles, FDA approval timing, or application sufficiency
- 34% involved alleged misrepresentations related to financial reporting
- 20% involved alleged misrepresentations in connection with M&A, IPOs, and other transactions

### Product Lifecycle Stage
- 57% of SCAs related to PRE-APPROVAL phase (Phase 1, 2, 2/3, 3, NDA, BLA, HDE)
- 43% related to POST-APPROVAL (regulatory issue, sales performance, financial reporting)

### Dismissal Success Rates (District Court)
- 2024: 59% (24 of 41)
- 2023: 68% (17 of 25)
- 2022: 52% (15 of 29)
- 2021: 58% (19 of 33)
- Companies won ALL 8 appellate cases in 2024

### Key Takeaway for D&O Underwriting
- Life sciences companies face rising lawsuits but maintain strong dismissal rates
- Evolving FDA, financial, and merger-related risks
- Pre-approval phase represents majority of claims
- Product efficacy/safety misrepresentation is the #1 allegation type

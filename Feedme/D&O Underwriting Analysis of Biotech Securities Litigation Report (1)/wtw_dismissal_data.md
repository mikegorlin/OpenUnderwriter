# WTW Life Sciences Litigation Dismissal Data (Multi-Year)

## Source: WTW 2025 Life Sciences Litigation Trends Report (Oct 2025)

### Dismissal Rates by Year (Life Sciences Companies):
| Year | Dismissals Won | Total Decisions | Dismissal Rate |
|------|---------------|-----------------|----------------|
| 2024 | 24 | 41 | 59% |
| 2023 | 17 | 25 | 68% |
| 2022 | 15 | 29 | 52% |

### Filing Data:
- SCA filings against life sciences companies increased 15% in 2024 over 2023
- Total of 50 cases in 2024 (WTW count, slightly different from Sidley's 44 due to methodology)
- Representing 22% of all SCAs filed

### Key Allegations in 2024 (WTW):
- FDA-related allegations remain dominant
- Product efficacy and safety misrepresentations: largest category
- Regulatory hurdles and timing: second largest
- Financial reporting: third

### D&O Market Conditions (WTW, Jan 2025):
- 2024 represented continued market improvement for D&O liability insurance
- Rate reductions continued but extent tapered
- Life sciences D&O rate predictions: low single digits
- Growing exposures on clean accounts leading to further reductions

### WTW Insurance Marketplace Realities 2025 - Life Sciences:
- Rate predictions remain in low single digits
- Clean accounts seeing even further reductions
- Product liability exposures growing

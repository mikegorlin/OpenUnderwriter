# Cornerstone Research Settlement Data for D&O Underwriting

## From Search Results and Press Releases:

### 2025 Settlements (Cornerstone Research, Feb 2026)
- Median settlement: $17.3 million (RECORD HIGH - 29-year high)
- 20% increase from previous year
- Highest since 1997

### 2024 Settlements (Cornerstone Research)
- Number of settlements: 88 (up 6% from 83 in 2023)
- Median settlement for '33 Act claims only: $10.3 million (26% decrease from 2023)
- Aggregate settlements: $3.8 billion
- Top 10 settlements accounted for ~60% of total

### H1 2025 Settlements (Cooley, Aug 2025)
- Median settlement value: $12.5 million
- Slightly down from inflation-adjusted $14.3 million in 2024
- AI and biotech cases continue to rise
- Uptick in alleged losses and average settlement values

### 2024 Filings (Cornerstone Research, Jan 2025)
- Overall filing volume: 225 filings (up from 215 in 2023)
- Core filings (excluding M&A): increased 14%
- AI-related filings more than doubled
- 1933 Act filings declined

### Gallagher (H1 2024)
- Median settlement for cases filed over past 10 years: $9 million (H1 2024)

## KEY SETTLEMENT TRENDS FOR BIOTECH D&O UNDERWRITING:
1. Settlement amounts are RISING - median hit 29-year high in 2025
2. Biotech/life sciences cases continue to be a significant portion of filings
3. AI-related filings are a new emerging category (doubled in 2024)
4. The gap between median and average settlements is widening (mega-settlements skew averages)
5. For biotech specifically: most cases that survive MTD settle in the $5M-$25M range for small-cap companies

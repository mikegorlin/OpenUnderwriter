# Goodwin - Securities Litigation Against Life Sciences 2024 YIR

## Key Statistics

### Filing Data
- 225 new SCA filings in federal/state courts in 2024 (up 5% from 215 in 2023)
- 220 total "core" filings in 2024 (up from 209 in 2023)
- 52 filed securities class actions against life sciences in 2024 (up from 40 in 2023)
- 40% higher than the long-run average from 1997-2023
- Life sciences = largest single sector for federal SCA filings

### Claims Types
- 198 core cases brought ONLY Section 10(b) claims in 2024 - highest on record
- 1933 Act cases (Section 11) fell from 32 in 2023 to 21 in 2024
- SPAC-related cases: down to 11 in 2024 (from peak of 33 in 2021)
- M&A class actions: only 5 in 2024

### Dismissal Rates
- 15% of 2023 cases dismissed by end of 2024 (77.5% still pending)
- 42.2% of 2022 cases dismissed by end of 2024
- Long-term trend: about half of life sciences SCAs dismissed by courts
- Courts dismissing majority of observed cases in 2024

### Key Litigation Triggers for Life Sciences
- Negative FDA feedback/actions
- Clinical trial delays, suspensions, terminations
- Unfavorable clinical data results
- Adverse events affecting patients
- Product recalls
- Manufacturing/supply challenges

### Key Judicial Themes
- Distinction between pure opinions/predictions (nonactionable) vs statements of historical fact
- Companies must maintain care when speaking about ongoing activities:
  - Revenue trends
  - Clinical trial results
  - Active FDA discussions
  - Active manufacturing challenges

### Geographic Concentration
- Most significant circuits: First, Second, Third, and Ninth
